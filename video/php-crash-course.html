Why Learn PHP in 2023?

PHP remains one of the most popular server-side scripting languages, powering platforms like WordPress, Facebook (originally), Wikipedia, and many more. Here's why you should learn PHP:

  • High Demand: PHP developers are consistently in demand
  • Easy to Learn: Great first language for backend development
  • Huge Community: Extensive documentation and support
  • Cost Effective: Open-source with low hosting requirements
  • Versatile: Powers everything from blogs to enterprise applications
