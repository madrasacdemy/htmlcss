India's Historical Timeline

Independence of India (1947)

On August 15, 1947, India gained independence from British rule.

Republic Day (1950)

India became a republic on January 26, 1950, with the adoption of the Constitution.

Green Revolution (1960s)

The Green Revolution transformed Indian agriculture, improving food production.

Pokhran Nuclear Test (1998)

India conducted successful nuclear tests in Pokhran, strengthening its defense.

Chandrayaan-3 Moon Landing (2023)

India made history by landing Chandrayaan-3 near the Moon's south pole.
