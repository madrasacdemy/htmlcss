Welcome to Our Website!

This is a demo page to show how you can easily integrate floating social share buttons with text and images. The social share buttons will remain visible on the right side of the page as you scroll down, making it easy for users to share content across social media platforms.

Why Social Sharing is Important

Social sharing is one of the most effective ways to increase traffic and reach a broader audience. By adding social share buttons to your website, you enable users to easily share your content with their network. This can lead to higher visibility, increased engagement, and even more sales or leads.

How It Works

The floating social share buttons are integrated into the website using simple HTML, CSS, and FontAwesome icons. When a user clicks on one of the social media icons, they will be directed to the respective platform's share page, allowing them to post your content with just a few clicks.
