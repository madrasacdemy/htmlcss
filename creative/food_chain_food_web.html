Understanding Energy Flow

Food chains and food webs illustrate how energy moves through ecosystems. They show the feeding relationships between organisms and how energy is transferred from one level to another.

Food Chain: Linear sequence showing who eats whom
Food Web: Complex network of interconnected food chains
Trophic Levels: Positions in the energy transfer hierarchy

Food Chain vs Food Web

Food Chain

  • Linear sequence of organisms
  • Shows single pathway of energy flow
  • Simple and easy to understand
  • Each organism has one predator/prey

Food Web

  • Complex network of interconnected chains
  • Shows multiple feeding relationships
  • More realistic representation
  • Organisms can have multiple food sources
